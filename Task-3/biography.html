About Me

Hello! I’m Hannah Faye P. Bayno, a freshman at the University of Mindanao, pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science. I am passionate about technology and driven by a desire to become a successful Software Engineer. I have a bit of a reserved personality but I can adapt to different situations and places. I am very eager to learn more about the things my course has to offer. An interesting fact about me is that I actually own and manage a small business on the side; a crochet business which is totally unrelated to my course but I believe it has equipped me with the essential skills I need for project management, communication, and customer service. I learned to be patient, detailed, and professional in dealing with problems that may arise which can prove useful professionally and in daily life.
